Why would you want a long-haired cat?

The long-haired cat is everything you need and more as a cat enthusiast! The massive plush of their long fur only brings you closer with your cat during daily grooming, cuddles, and fur EVERYWHERE!

  • Like cuddling with a teddy bear!
  • They're way cuter when they play
  • Time to spend with your cat daily


However, long-haired cats are not great for humans with allergies because they are the the epicenter for pet dander and loose fur. If you don't have a lot of time to dedicate to grooming your cat, long-haired cats may not be for you.'

Balinese - /bah-luh-neez/
