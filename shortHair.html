Why would you want a short-haired cat?

The short-haired cat is a great companion for those who don't have much time to brush out lots of fur! Some of these cats are also hypoallergenic as well.

  • Great for allergies
  • Less vacuuming!
  • Less brushing


However, fur is still going to be shed and depending on the cat, there could be a LOT of shedding. Keep that in mind when seeking a new companion!
