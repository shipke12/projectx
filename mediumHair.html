Why would you want a medium-haired cat?

Medium-haired cats can give you the fluffy plush fur that you crave without the tedious daily grooming of long-haired cats!

  • Fluffy cuddle buddy!
  • Brushing only sometimes!
  • They have more fur than the short-haired!


However, if you have an allergy to cats, it's best you stay away from medium-haired cats. They are a slippery slope to long-haired cats in terms of pet dander and fur!

American Wirehair - /əˈmerəkən wahyuh r-hair/

Usual Colors: Can come in almost any possible cat coat color combinations, tend to have a lighter color underbelly and come in tabby color pattern.

Usual Behavior: Love to sleep on or around their owners and are always very playful. They have been described to have an almost humorous personality.



Bengal - /ben-gawl/

Usual Colors: Bengal cats can come in a variety of colors but always in a spotted or marble pattern. Most commonly come in Brown Spotted, Seal Lynx Point (snow), Sepia, silver, and Mink Spotted Tabby Bengals.

Usual Behavior: Always very smart and energetic. Tend to be very playful with their owners and love the outdoors. They are a very specific breed of cat that loves to play in water.

Cyprus - /sahy-pruh s/

Usual Colors: Can come in many different colors and patterns but almost always have a white or lighter colored underbelly.

Usual Behavior: Are always very athletic and energetic. Descended from cats originating in mountainous regions so love to play outdoors.



Oriental - /awr-ee-en-tl/

Usual Colors: Comes in over 300 different colors and patterns. The most common are Solid, Shaded, Smoke, Parti-color, Tabby, and Bicolor patterns.

Usual Behavior: Very active and curious cats. Love to play and are generally not very shy.

Tonkinese - /tawng-kin-eez/

Usual Colors: Very similar to a Siamese cat’s coat and colors. Almost always cream colored with dark colored tips.

Usual Behavior: Very vocal and active, similar to Siamese cats. Are always very intelligent and form a strong bond with their owners.



Turkish Van - /tur-kish van/

Usual Colors: Can come in many varieties of colors but most commonly are seen with solid color lighter coats.

Usual Behavior: From early bloodlines this cat has developed into a very good hunter. The more domesticated cats are very social and friendly, especially towards their owners. Because of their hunting skills they love to play and be active outside. Also very friendly towards strangers and other animals.
