Why would you want a medium cat?

The hairless cat is a great companion for those who are allergic to cats but still love them anyway. Without all that fur, they secrete less of the proteins that triggers allergic reactions in some people!

  • Great for allergies
  • Less vacuuming!
  • No brushing


However, this isn't just a pet rock that you never have to care for either. Due to their exposed skin, they need regular bathing, watch their sun exposure, they're a LOT more expensive than other cats, and they eat a lot more due to their metabolisms.
